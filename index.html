About Ataxia

Cerebellar ataxia is one of a group of genetic disorders characterised by slowly progressive incoordination of gait and is often associated with poor coordination of hands, speech and eye movements .

Social Significance

Of all the neurodegenerative disorders known and studied, hereditary cerebellar ataxia still remains an unsolved problem. The diagnosis is usually done using the patient's medical history, family history and a complete neurological evaluation through MRI Scan followed by number of genetic tests. Such procedures are rather expensive and time consuming. With so many problems to solve, this project aims to create a platform for clinicians to diagnose a patient's disorder in a cost and time effective manner. We aim to attain our motive by gathering and curating the data into a database that can be easily accessed. Also the number of genes to be targeted will be narrowed down and the patient will get a better and rapid diagnosis in lesser time.
